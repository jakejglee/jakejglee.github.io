vileplume - the planter box for everyone

After finishing school at UC Santa Cruz in 2017, two engineer friends of mine, Lon and Harry, and I decided to set up an aeroponics grow tent. Our goal was to use sensor data fed into a Raspberry Pi to intelligently regulate plant growth conditions, and ultimately experiment with growing different types of plants (cannabis, microgreens, and radishes). We worked on the project for three summer months after graduation and were able to get all of the individual components working, but stopped short of integrating each component.

Several years later, we met again and Lon had kept all of the project's components in storage. Our goal is still to finish our initial aeroponics project, but ultimately to expand on the basic idea of a grow box and create a modular box that can be altered for either aeroponics, hydroponics, or soil growth as easily as can reasonably be accomplished.
