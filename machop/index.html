background

It is hard to make anything without access to a nice workspace. As my housemate and I reclaimed our cluttered garage for the valuable space, we disassembled large wooden structures that left us with a ton of scrap wood. For a month in the middle of summer, we kept it outside uncovered. San Jose had been so dry, there was no way that wood would warp...we thought.

Then came the rain.

Ultimately, we wound up with a pile of pretty warped wood. As a sustainability enthusiast, I couldn't just let that wood go to waste. When Life gives you warped wood, you build a workbench.
